Synthetic Face for Intelligent Personal Assistant

Hi! my name is Vishal Yadav and this website has been created to do a survey for my dissertation in accordance with the requirement of the degree (Master of Science in the Faculty of Engineering).

This website has been temporarily created to gather survey data on a fast pace. The aim of the survey is to capture a score on a scale of 0-10 for the attractiveness of a human face. The scores punched by the users will be used to train the computer to score attractiveness like normal human being.

Please read below instructions carefully before proceeding.

  • All the facial images used in this website are taken from 4 different databases[1][2][3][4]. People have voluntarily posed for the research work.
  • Each database has been downloaded on license terms. All the images will solely be used for the purpose of research and any practice involves copying of these images from the website can land you in a legal trouble.
